Turning Ideas Into Reality





An Individualistic Approach To Game Development


In a rapidly changing industry, we are here to revolutionize the idea of game development by adapting and making the most of new technologies. We have come a long way from using the traditional game development methods by working with cutting edge academic research to prototype and test new ideas in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Augmented Reality.




The Next Step In Augmented Reality


Having limitless applications, AR certainly is at the top of our list of technologies for game and mobile application development. We are constantly researching building prototypes, integrating AR with most of our games.

With applications across all fields, we are tirelessly developing working models of AR integrated applications.




Bringing Virtual Reality To Actual Reality


The next giant leap in application development is the introduction of VR.Virtual Reality takes the user on an epic journey of the senses by transporting them to a new world altogether.
We aim to introduce VR to the world of mechanical sciences as well as the world of game development, urging our users to experience new bounds of technology.
